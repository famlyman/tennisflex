

Welcome to Tennis Flex!
Fresh balls, fair calls, and a schedule that fits it all. 

Finally, a league that works as hard as your cross-court forehand!
We know the struggle: you want to play, but life gets in the way. That’s why we built Tennis Flex, the league designed for the everyday player who loves the game but hates the rigid calendar. No more forfeited matches or stressful coordination—just pure tennis on your terms.

Why You’ll Love It:
Your Game, Your Time: Big sets, zero nerves, and the schedule you deserve. You connect with local players and book your matches whenever (and wherever) it works for you.

Real Play, Real Easy: Great matches on a flexible day—flex your game with Tennis Flex! Whether you’re a weekend warrior or a weeknight post-work hitter, we find you the right level of competition without the "pro" pressure.

The Flexible League: We’ve stripped away the overhead so you can focus on the rally. It’s about finding your rhythm, meeting new hitting partners, and getting those sets in without checking your watch every five minutes.

Ready to play? Grab your racquet and let’s get moving. Tennis Flex: The Flexible League.

How it works!!
Registration opens for a given season, you register for that season within the division you wish to play, registration closes, matchup s are made and you have until the close of the season to complete matches. All incomplete matches will not be counted and no points or rating adjustments will be made.
